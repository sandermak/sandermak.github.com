Sander Mak

Sander is a Fellow at Luminis in The Netherlands, where he crafts modular and scalable software, most often on the JVM, but with a touch of TypeScript when needed. He also is a Java Champion and author of the O'Reilly book 'Java 9 Modularity' (see javamodularity.com). As an avid conference speaker, Sander loves sharing knowledge, also through his blog at branchandbound.net and as Pluralsight instructor.

Pluralsight Courses

Pluralsight Course: Java 9 Modularity

This two hour video course gives you insight into the Java module system using practical examples. You'll learn how to create and use modules and how to migrate your applications to Java 9.
